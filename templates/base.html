About

Create an account, enter basic information your marketing campaigns, including the cost and start date. Include the number of qualified marketing and converted sales leads each campaign has generated, then let us give you the cost per marketing lead and converted lead, giving you a clear overview of which campaigns are yielding the best results.

group

How to use

Without worrying about email copy, or which hashtags to use, entering only the numbers allows you to focus on the value of each campaign. Enter the number of leads and cost, we provide the cost per lead and conversion rate, and from there you can make qualified, strategic marketing decisions.

settings

Wondering how you compare?

Wonder whether your leads cost you more than the competition? Take a look at comparative data from other companies. All data is confidential, so you can only see an average per industry, but it will give you an idea on how your activities are performing.
