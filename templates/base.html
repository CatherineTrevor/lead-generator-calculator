About

Plan your marketing strategy and budget allocation using real data. Lead Generation Calculator allows you to monitor the total cost and leads generated by your marketing campaigns and access the cost per marketing lead and converted sales lead. A clear and simple overview of which campaigns are yielding the best results for you.

settings

How to use

Open a free account, enter the information about each marketing campaign you wish to monitor, and we provide the cost per lead and conversion rate. From there you can make qualified, strategic marketing decisions that work for you. Just because an activity yields plenty of leads, doesn't mean it gives you the best value.

group

The competition

Wonder whether your leads cost you more than the competition? Take a look at comparative data from other companies to get an idea. All data is confidential, so you can only see an average per industry, but it will give you an idea on how your activities are performing and whether other communication platforms would perform better.
